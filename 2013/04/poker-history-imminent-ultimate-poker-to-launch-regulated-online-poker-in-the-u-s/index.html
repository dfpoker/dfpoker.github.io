Poker History Imminent – Ultimate Poker to Launch Regulated Online Poker in the U.S.

Posted on April 30, 2013 by Diamond Flush

History is being made today as Ultimate Poker is launching legal real money online poker games at 12 noon ET. The site will be the first to launch in the United States with a legal nod from Nevada regulators.  Nevada approved their final amended bill allowing online poker in February.

Ultimate Poker is majority owned by Station Casinos LLC (Frank and Lorenzo Fertitta of Fertitta Interactive), who operate 16 brick and mortar casinos in Southern Nevada. Ultimate Gaming acquired California based CyberArts in 2011 and therefore owns its own software platform to be utilized for gaming.

Any person over the age of 21 and physically located within Nevada boundaries will be permitted to play for real money on the site. Persons registering on the site will be required to provide their social security number, their identity, age, address, mobile phone number and contact details, including a valid e-mail address, their place of residence, and relevant payment information. Verification of each player’s identity and their physical location at time of play will be implemented.

Players will be able to deposit or cash out in person any any one of the Station Casino locations in Nevada.  Online deposits will also be accepted via e-checks, bank wires and check by mail. Daily and monthly limits on deposit amounts will be implemented, dependant on player history, deposit method and account status. According to their website, limits on withdrawals will be imposed as well, with similar criteria being considered.

Ultimate Poker is the exclusive partner of the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC). A few weeks ago, the poker site signed professional poker player Antonio Esfandiari as their ambassador.
